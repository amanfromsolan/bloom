## New
- Drag the sidebar's edge to resize it — Enso remembers your width.
- The space icon picker is all new: search nearly 200 icons and every emoji, each in its own grid, styled to match the rest of Enso.
- The shuffle button got a playful spring — icons blur out and pop in as you roll the dice.
- Collapse or expand all folders in a space with one click, right next to the space menu.
- ⌘-click a link in the terminal to open it in your browser — the cursor becomes a pointer so you know it's live.
- New Terminal in Current Folder now lands where you are: inside the same sidebar folder, right next to your current tab.

## Improved
- Quitting now asks with a proper dialog — click Quit or Cancel, press ⌘Q again, or hit Esc. No more guessing.
- The terminal's rounded corners now nest with the window's curve, so the frame reads as one shape.
- The terminal sits flush against the sidebar for a cleaner seam.
- The Ghostty config button in Settings opens the file in your text editor instead of revealing the folder.

## Fixed
- Double-clicking the title bar area now zooms the window, as macOS intends.
- The three-dot menu icon in the space header is properly centered.
